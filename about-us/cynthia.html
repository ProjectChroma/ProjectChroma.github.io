Bio: Cynthia

Cynthia is a passionate Computer Science student at Liberty. He passed Intro to Computer Science last year with flying colors, and is currently achieving similar honors in AP Computer Science and Web Design.

Cynthia has a wide variety of past experience with computer science. He picked up GameMaker and learned GML (GameMaker Language) as a side project over the summer after his 6th grade, learned Alice over the summer after 7th, and followed with Java after 8th. After discovering Github (and Git in the process), Cynthia set up a thriving Github account and continues to maintain over 25 active projects there.

Cynthia is also President and two-time Student Chief of Software for the Iron Patriots, Liberty's FRC robotics team. Cynthia was responsible for all of the robot's code during the Aerial Assist (2014) and Recycle Rush (2015) competitions.
